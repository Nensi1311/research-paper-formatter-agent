ScholarEnv is the first OpenEnv RL environment for AI-assisted peer review and
scholarly integrity verification. Built on PRS (arxiv 2512.07478), PBRS (Ng et al.
1999), AdaRFT (arxiv 2504.05520), Veri-R1 (arxiv 2510.01932), and RLVE (arxiv 2511.07317).
